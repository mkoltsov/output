Free does not mean unlimited

Spark is excellent for launch, demos, and modest apps. The common mistakes are using paid Google Cloud products accidentally, depending on SMS auth, or ignoring database reads.

!
Watch

Reads can spike

Realtime listeners and broad Firestore queries can consume reads quickly. Use indexes, pagination, and narrow listeners.

Budget with reads, not users

Blaze can bill

Blaze includes no-cost quotas, but overages can charge the linked billing account. Budget alerts are alerts, not hard caps.

Set alerts before launch
