Practical Family & Packing Notes (Metric)

  • Daily walking + standing often 8–14 km on central days; comfortable shoes essential.
  • June London weather: typical 15–23 °C daytime, cooler evenings, showers possible any day. Layers + compact rain jacket or umbrella.
  • Hotel breakfast is a major money and time saver — use it fully.
  • Many attractions have family trails or shorter “highlights” routes; do not feel obliged to see everything.
  • Stroller access is generally good at museums; Tower of London has steps and some lifts — plan accordingly.
  • Power banks + multi-USB charger for phones (tickets, maps, photos).
  • Small daypack with water, snacks, wet wipes, basic first aid, and a light fleece.
