Important limitations

  • Shoe comparisons are exact size and colour matches: adidas EU 43⅓ / US 9.5, Nike EU 41 / US 8, and Under Armour EU 46 / US 12.
  • For Casio and Timex watches, the final letters are regional market suffixes; the compared case, dial, strap/bracelet, movement and functions match.
  • Physical games have the same title, platform and edition but region-specific ratings and packaging. Verify language preferences before buying.
  • LEGO US delivery uses the currently published standard tiers: $4.95 up to $25.00 and $6.95 from $25.01 to $34.99. Free US delivery begins at $35.00.
  • The two sub-65 zł Amazon.pl LEGO rows include a conservative 19.99 zł Polish delivery allowance because the standard charge was deferred to checkout. Their pages also displayed free delivery for a first order, which would improve the result for eligible buyers.
  • LEGO 77262 was accepted because the US page had an active Backorder control and stated shipment by 23 July 2026; it was not treated as immediately in stock.
  • US sales tax is excluded. Adding sales tax would increase the US side and therefore increase the stated Polish saving.
  • Many other Pepper leads failed because a page was stale, sold out, lacked an exact US counterpart, depended on another size/colour, or was not cheaper after delivery. They were not used to pad the table.
