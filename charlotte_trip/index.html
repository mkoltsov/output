Do Before Leaving

Buy/book Titanic first. Fever says late entry is not permitted and recommends booking ahead because box-office tickets can sell out.

Reserve Micro Center pickup. The Pi 400 is the best known deal; don’t rely on stock still being there late afternoon if it matters.

Bring trunk space. IKEA turns small “just looking” trips into boxes. Keep blankets or straps if you might buy furniture.

Fuel math: at ~349 miles and ~31 mpg highway, expect ~11.3 gallons. With Charlotte-area regular around $4/gal recently, budget about $46 fuel.
