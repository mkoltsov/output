Why This Beats The Old Route

Old route: Cary → IKEA → Titanic → GRID → Micro Center → Cary. It burns the north-side IKEA advantage at the beginning, then sends you across Charlotte and back out from the south side.

Better route: Cary → Titanic → Micro Center → The GRID → IKEA → Cary. It handles the timed attraction first, takes the closer south-side Micro Center stop next, swings west to The GRID, then finishes north at IKEA before the long drive home.

Rejected alternative: Cary → Titanic → The GRID → Micro Center → IKEA → Cary. That skips the closer Micro Center leg after Titanic and creates avoidable crossing/backtracking inside Charlotte.
