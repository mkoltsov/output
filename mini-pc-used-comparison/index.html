How to use the prices

Bold used ranges are target purchase prices, not claims that stock is continuously available. Values without an asterisk are anchored by observed completed sales or live regional listings. Asterisks mark informed fair-buy estimates where comparable used supply was too sparse. EU prices normally include VAT when sold by a business; US prices normally exclude sales tax.

  • Below $300 / €350: buy an HP EliteDesk 800 G6 Mini. It is the value winner for non-gaming use.
  • Around $350–500 / €400–550: prioritise a Beelink SER8 or Minisforum UM790 Pro for the much faster Radeon 780M.
  • Above $750 / €850: an AI X1 Pro only makes sense when OCuLink, three SSD slots or very high CPU performance will actually be used.
  • For used systems, require clear photographs, the original correct-wattage power adapter, an unlocked BIOS, working Wi-Fi/Bluetooth and a return window. Check SSD health, memory errors, every USB-C/USB4 port, Ethernet and sleep/wake behaviour immediately.
  • Business minis often ship without Wi-Fi antennas or storage caddies. Consumer minis may have shorter firmware-support histories. Price those omissions before buying.
