SKADIS PVC Drying Rack Concept

Two printed SKADIS load pegs carry long PVC side rails. Repeated printed saddle mounts clamp to those rails and hold shorter PVC cross pipes as the clothes rack bars.

Printable Parts Shown

  • 2x SKADIS peg plates with pipe-insert spigots
  • 10x saddle T-mounts for 5 crossbars
  • 2x long PVC side rails, cut to depth
  • 5x shorter PVC drying bars, cut to width

1/2 in PVC assumption 240 mm rail spacing 430 mm rack depth M2.5 T-nut style anchors

Verified Inputs And Assumptions

Verified
IKEA lists SKADIS as a wall/desk pegboard and warns it must be securely anchored.
Verified
Published SKADIS T-nut docs give a 40 x 40 mm pattern, second pattern offset 20 mm, and 5 x 15 mm slots.
Assumed
Nominal 1/2 in Schedule 40 PVC: about 21.3 mm OD, using the common 0.840 in OD reference. Measure your pipe ID before sizing the printed spigots.
Not rated
This is a visualization, not a tested load-bearing model. Wet clothes can overload a pegboard mount quickly.
