Fold-Down SKADIS PVC Clothesline Rack

A printed SKADIS hinge pair carries a fold-out PVC perimeter frame. The drying surface is cord, not PVC crossbars, with printed comb caps and tie-off points to keep the lines evenly spaced.

Printable Parts Shown

  • 2x SKADIS hinge/peg blocks with internal PVC spigots
  • 4x frame corner caps with cord eyelets/tie-offs
  • PVC perimeter only: rear, front, and two side rails
  • 6x clothesline cord runs across the fold-out frame
