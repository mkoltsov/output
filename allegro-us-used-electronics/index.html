Method that must be used for the next scan

  1. Match the exact manufacturer part number, options, accessories and test state—not merely the product family.
  2. Prefer recent sold evidence. If only current asks exist, label the result unverified and haircut the expected exit price.
  3. For the screening calculation, deduct 13.25% selling costs plus realistic insured outbound shipping. Taxes, returns, import friction and working capital are still additional downside.
  4. Require at least US $150 and 15% expected profit after those costs before calling anything an actionable resale buy.
  5. Keep own-use replacement value separate from resale value. Authorised new/dealer pricing can justify buying a spare, but cannot justify an arbitrage claim.
