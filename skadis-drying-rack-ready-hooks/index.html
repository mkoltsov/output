Ready-Hook SKADIS PVC Clothesline Rack

The rack uses ready-to-print SKADIS hookset parts from Thingiverse for every board connection. Everything structural is PVC: perimeter pipe, ready elbows, cord lines, and two white angled PVC supports under the rack.

Printable Parts Shown

  • 4x ready Thingiverse SKADIS hookset pegs, reused as all board mounts
  • 2x white PVC angled support pipes under the rack
  • PVC perimeter with ready elbows and simple angled fittings
  • 6x clothesline cord runs across the frame

Verified Inputs And Assumptions

Observed
The provided reference photo shows a wall-hinged PVC rectangle with blue parallel cord lines for clothes.
Changed
The load-bearing members are now white PVC tubes underneath the rack, using simple angled PVC fittings.
Thingiverse
Primary mount: CSD_Salzburg's IKEA SKADIS Hookset, thing 2849369. Lower hooks can also be swapped for secure hook/plug thing 4604087 or round hook thing 7071556.
Verified
IKEA lists SKADIS as a wall/desk pegboard and warns it must be securely anchored.
Verified
Published SKADIS slot docs give a 40 x 40 mm pattern, second pattern offset 20 mm, and 5 x 15 mm slots; this concept uses ready hook pegs instead of T-nuts.
Assumed
Nominal 1/2 in Schedule 40 PVC: about 21.3 mm OD, using the common 0.840 in OD reference.
Not rated
This is a visualization, not tested load-bearing CAD. SKADIS and the wall attachment must be checked for wet laundry loads before printing.
Sources
